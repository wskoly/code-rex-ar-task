Face Anchor Reference Guide
Common Anchor Points:
  • 10: Top of head (perfect for hats)
  • 168: Center of face between eyes (good for glasses)
  • 9: Nose tip
  • 0-16: Face outline/jawline
  • 33, 133: Eye corners
  • 61, 291: Mouth corners
Tips for Anchor Selection:
  • Hats work best with top head anchors (8-10)
  • Glasses need stable eye-area anchors (168, 6)
  • Lower numbers = more stable tracking
  • Test with different face angles
  • Consider accessory weight distribution
